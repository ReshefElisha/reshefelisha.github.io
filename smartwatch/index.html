Sports Smartwatch

Read More

As a personal project, I decided to design a smartwatch specifically for outdoor sports and water activities. I noticed that there are no smartwatches that address the scenario in which the user’s hand may not be completely free. Personally I’d like to be able to use a smartwatch when I’m out surfing or when I’m skiing. However, most smartwatches aren’t waterproof and have small buttons that don’t work well with gloves; I have to go watch-less during those activities.

The first requirement I wanted to solve was waterproofing the watch so it could be used in water sports. To accomplish this, I had to first get rid of the charging port, and make some space for a wireless charging antenna instead. I also had to place the screen behind a relatively thick layer of protective glass. These two features combined call for the main body of the watch, the center circle, to be thicker than in most watches. To maintain the illusion of a thinner body, I placed the four buttons of the watch further away from the center, and on thinner “wings” on either side of the body. These give the watch a flatter design.

The second requirement that needed to be addressed was the use of the watch with gloves. Current smartwatches place the buttons inside grooves in order to hide them or make them blend into the watch’s body. I went the opposite route, by making the buttons stick out, and easy to find even without looking. The three menu buttons are easily accessible by the thumb, even when it is covered in a glove. They are wide enough to not require any special dexterity to use. The main power/home button was put on the right and enlarged so it would be easy to hit even when the watch isn’t fully outside of one’s sleeve (this design assumes a right handed user that would wear the watch on their left hand, thus the left side of the watch is in fact facing towards the sleeve, while the right faces towards the back of the hand).

These design choices, combined with rigid materials for the body and straps, should make for a very user-friendly yet stylish sports smartwatch.
